Guidance

Using the quiz

The grey area and Carry row responds to mouse clicks. Clicking once will set it to "1", clicking again set it to "0". (You can just fill in the "1"s and use the button).

You can also use "1" "0" and "c" on the keyboard to type the answer from the right

Then click to check it. If you have not filled in the 0's that will be offered (but will happen automatically after a delay). If there are problems with your answer will appear, click it for in-depth guidance. Then click to move on (again this will also happen automatically after a delay)

Finishing the exercise

The questions will get harder as you go. When you have praciticed enough online please click the button, and print out the work sheet to do on paper. And hand that in asap.

Doing the questions

Remember to work from the right, adding one column at a time (and marking the carry row of the next column to the left if you need to)
